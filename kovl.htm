KOVL Radio

July 2017 – Present

Viewing Info

View KOVL Radio

Exchanging ideas and contextualizing feedback with the management of KOVL Radio produced a website which satisfied management’s expectations.

In July of 2017, the newly formed management team of KOVL Radio commissioned a website.

KOVL Radio is one of the premier online radio stations based in St. Louis, MO. Personalities provide hours of conversations with guests, compelling inviting their audience to tune in. Some of the best R&B and hip-hop music accompanies conversations throughout the broadcast day.

KOVL Radio’s nascent operations required an online presence which reflected professionalism and polish while functioning as effectively in a desktop browser as well as mobile devices.

The management of KOVL Radio initiated a number of conversations between them and myself. The conversations were productive and the information they shared informed the wireframes and mockups created later.

The development period allowed for learning responsive design concepts and ramping up my CSS layout skills.

As the process from wireframe to working site continued, I kept in touch with the management. During milestones, they would offer their feedback on the work done so far. After receiving those notes, fine-tuning the alpha version of the site into a more pleasing site was the next step.

The site successfully launched in January of 2018.

In the timespan since the site’s launch, the management periodically asked for refinements to the UI and branding.

With each refinement, the site better serves the needs of the listeners of KOVL Radio.

 
 

Takeaways

I envision many young or junior freelancers struggle with their self-assurance while communicating with clients. I know I struggle myself.

In my case, I wonder if my clients misread or misunderstand questions at the planning stage. Or that I misunderstand the business needs of my clients or underappreciate the importance of their online presence to the health of their business.

But with every successful project, I get more information to inform my mental model of the worries or expectations which my clients have of me and their websites.
